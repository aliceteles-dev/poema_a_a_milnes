Meu poema favorito

  • Now we are six

    When I was one,
    I had just begun.

    When I was two,
    I was nearly new.

    When I was three,
    I was hardly me.

    When I was four,
    I was not much more.

    When I was five,
    I was just alive.

    But now I am six,
    I'm as clever as clever,

    So I think I'll be six now
    for ever and ever.

    A. A. Milnes

  • Agora temos seis

    Quando eu tinha um ano,
    eu estava apenas começando.

    Quando eu tinha dois,
    Eu era quase novo.

    Quando eu tinha três,
    Eu mal era eu.

    Quando eu tinha quatro,
    Eu já não era muita coisa.

    Quando eu tinha cinco,
    Eu estava apenas vivo.

    Mas agora eu tenho seis,
    Sou tão inteligente quanto se pode ser,

    Então acho que vou ter seis agora
    e para todo sempre.

    A. A. Milnes
